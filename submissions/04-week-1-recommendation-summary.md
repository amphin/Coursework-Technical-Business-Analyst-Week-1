Legacy-Trust should adopt the following automation opportunities: 
- prioritize OP-02 (case-activity synchronisation)
- OP-05 (rules-based routing)
- OP-03 (self-serve account summary).

OP-02 is the strongest first recommendation because it addresses the core data reliability and duplicate-handling issues that undermine collections performance and reporting trust. OP-05 is the second recommendation because it creates clearer separation between straightforward and specialist cases, improving flow efficiency and reducing avoidable escalation. OP-03 is the third recommendation because it improves customer visibility of balances and progress, reducing repeat contacts and supporting faster resolution for eligible cases. This ranking is consistent with the strongest evidence from discovery, JTBD priorities, pain-point mapping, BPMN analysis, and ROI outputs. Together, these three recommendations provide the highest-value combination of operational impact and customer benefit.

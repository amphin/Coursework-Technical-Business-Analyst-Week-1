### Situation
RetailCo is an e-commerce company which has recently migrated its PIM to a SaaS platform.

### Complication

The RetailCo website is currently interacting with both the old and new PIM in its operation, resulting in mismatched data.

### Question
What constitutes the process for a product to move from being added to the system, to being displayed for customers on the website?

### Answer
To map a single process for product movement, unifying all the current parallel approaches.

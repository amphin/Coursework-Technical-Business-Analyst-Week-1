### Situation
A DfE inspection found that Merton Cross Borough Council could not produce sufficient evidence to show whether EHCP assessments were complete in the statutory 20-week timeframe.

### Complication
Investigation revealed widespread data quality issues and inconsistent record keeping, making it unclear whether the problem was technological or organisational.

### Question
How can the council quickly address the inspection concerns while ensuring the underlying causes are resolved?

### Answer
Recover and validate missing EHCP data to address compliance risks, while conducting a structured review of processes and systems to prevent the problem recurring.

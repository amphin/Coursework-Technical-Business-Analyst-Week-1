### Situation
Severn Vale ICS has requested all organisations, of which Northfield NHS Trust is one, to achieve HL7 7HIR compliance by the end of this fiscal year, in order to share patient data through a regional care record platform with the objective of providing more coordinated care across the region.

### Complication
Northfield's systems are not fully ready to integrate in accordance with compliance requirements, due to undefined governance responsibilities and poorly understood data flows.

### Question
What must Northfield do to safely deliver the required data sharing capability.

### Answer
Establish governance and data-sharing requirements from the ICS, then determine the technical solution needed to achieve FHIR compliance and integration.
